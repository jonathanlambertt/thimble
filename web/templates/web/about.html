About

What is Thimble?

Thimble is a mobile app for group sharing. Create groups for anything you want and add your friends to them. In groups you can share updates, links and photos. React to posts by sending an emoji.

Who made this?

Thimble is founded by Jonathan Lambert and Patrick Mucsi.
